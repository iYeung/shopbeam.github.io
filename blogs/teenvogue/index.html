Spring's Coolest Print Might Actually Make You Feel Warmer

Consider your (extended) winter blues cured.

by Sarah Brody
  • Share on Tumblr

Spring is officially here (somewhere, we think), and while we're itching to swap out our bulky sweaters in favor of barely-there spaghetti straps, it seems as though we might need to hold onto our layers just a little longer. But fear not fellow warm weather enthusiasts, because in order to both cure cabin fever and jumpstart our seasonal closet clean-out, we've gone ahead and picked some of our favorite pieces to keep you ahead of what's sure to be this season's most popular trend: tropical-inspired prints.

From Kenzo to Fausto Puglisi to Farfetch, fashion's much-needed update on Aloha attire dominated the spring runways and has been keeping us warm all winter long. For a lei'ed-back take on the trend, look no further than blogger Priscila above, who's turning up the heat in a tropical top with denim cutoffs and reflective sunglasses. Dare to be bold? Create the ultimate island motif ensemble by pairing palm print on print (on print) with a fitted crop top, flared skirt, and sneakers in varying colorways.

See below to view our easy breezy must-haves that will transition you from what's been a never-ending winter into what we hope is an endless summer.
