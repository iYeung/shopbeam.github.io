Chrissy Teigen, And The Hottest Babes From 2014′s Guys’ Choice Awards

While it was supposed to be the fellas’ night to shine at Spike’s Guys’ Choice Awards in Culver City on Saturday, it was the ladies who showed UP for the event, looking all sorts of fierce. Spike TV’s annual comedy awards show, which are dedicated to all things bro-y, brought out the industries foinest, from mega-babe Chrissy Teigen, to the very vampy, Jessica Alba. Rihanna also showed up to accept her award for ‘Most Desirable Woman’ (shocker), and even decided to wear clothes for the occasion. Peep the red carpet’s elite below, and check out the awards when the air on Wednesday, June 11th, to see how it all went down.
