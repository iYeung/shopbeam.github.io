Brooklyn Leather Studded High-Top Sneaker

By Masa - September 16th, 2013
0

There is no denying that Chad Muska became more avant garde as his style and personality progressed in life. His pro sneaker from Suede Low-Top Sneaker, is reflective of his journey as well. This fourth installment is a mixture of styles from the past, the present, and the future, as Muska describes it. Combining elements from Sci-Fi films, basketball shoes, and high fashion footwear, these shoes make for an interesting design. Best of all, these are still intended to be skated in, making these a super versatile sneaker. EVA heel cushioning absorbs shock from ollies over huge gaps or stairs.
