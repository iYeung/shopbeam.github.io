Blue Blogger Heaven

This sexy and sophisticated Zoe Boutique dress is worn by fashionistas all over, such as bloggers Aimee Song and Sandra Hagelstam, as well as actresses Amanda Seyfried and Ashley Madekwe. We love to round up the look with this Soho Python Clutch from Gucci, which is perfect for day to night.

Delicate blue lace is set on top of layered mesh fabric, giving the illusion of skin (think: any thing JLo has ever worn on the red carpet). The nude foundation makes the royal blue details pop, and the strategic print comes in at the waist, creating an hourglass effect that is super sexy. This is a great way to make a major style statement that is perfect for summer.

To make the look especially your own, or to change the style of the dress for more versatility, simply add accessories! Check out some of the celebs that have rocked it below:


Some suggestions on ways to change up your look: add a necklace and sunglasses for a cool daytime look; add a blazer and a closed toe shoe for a more conservative (but still sexy) look; add red platform sandals and lipstick for a sexy nighttime look.

What is your favorite way to wear this dress?
