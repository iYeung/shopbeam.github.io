        Whether you're ready or not, spring clothes are trickling in. Like little rays of sunshine at your favorite retail destinations, these threads are glimmers of hope that warmer days are ahead. To help you get out from underneath the sartorial rock otherwise known as your dark-colored cardigan/hoodie/puffer jacket, we've highlighted a few spring items you'll likely add to your wardrobe the moment the thermostat hits a suitable, outerwear-shedding temp.

        Since there's little doubt that these spring pieces will be coming soon to a closet near you, let us ruminate on how to maximize your wardrobe to get the most out of each of them — and everything else you own. Considering both the new items worth the buy, and those warm-weather mainstays you should bring out of storage, here are a few suggestions on how to wear spring styles safe or play them up. Get excited, people: We're almost there.

        Whether you enjoyed a few-too-many holiday confections (hey, who didn't?), are blinded by the sight of your own pasty skin, or just prefer to maintain a degree of modesty, a teeny-weeny bikini may not be appearing on your wish list for spring. But, that doesn't mean you can't swimsuit-up and still look fantastic.

        Enter the tankini. Often providing even more coverage than a one-piece, this tank-and-bottom combo can be a godsend if you're a beach-lover who's not so keen on baring her belly. And, because we'd never steer your style wrong, we've gathered up nine options that deliver full coverage, and fashion, too. Click through, but don't be alarmed if you find yourself getting excited to swimsuit-shop for the first time since puberty. It may be a foreign feeling, but it's a good one.
