Spring Break Essentials: SHOP Outfits & Accessories For Your Trip

Fri, March 7, 2014 4:49pm EDT by HL Intern 3 Comments
Courtesy
View Gallery
34 Photos

Ready for some fun in the sun?  Don’t jet off without checking off our list of spring break essentials! From trendy accessories to flirty outfits and classic staples, you can’t go wrong scooping up these looks! So be sure to pack your passport, sunscreen, and camera — and start shopping.

Spring break is just around the corner and we are here to help you pack! It gets a little overwhelming, sorting through all your winter sweaters and digging out last summer’s bathing suits — so let us save you the hassle! Relaxing mode starts now, read on and check out everything you will need for the best sun-packed spring break!

Spring Break Outfits 2014:

We can already hear the sun and sand calling your name, but first let’s decide how to sport the best vacation looks so you can be sure to rock your spring break in style! First things first,check out some funky geometric-style swimsuits to really make a splash by the pool!

From flirty dresses and bikinis to denim shorts and sandals, you don’t need much to get ready for your trip! Have fun and incorporate how new trends into your spring break wardrobe by rocking florals or crop tops — you’re sure to turn heads when you step out in style.

We also recommend packing a pair of jeans or a jumpsuit — you never know when the temperature will drop! Flaunt the cutest sheer coverups for a walk on the beach and don’t forget to grab your gladiator sandals to preserve your perfectly painted bright pedicure. For a stroll by the boardwalk shops, rock some patterned colored shorts and a simple tee to look totally casual and cool.

Simply shop our picks and follow our tips and get ready to have a fabulous, (fashionable!), time!
